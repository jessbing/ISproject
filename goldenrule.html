The Golden Rule

The key to sucess no matter where you are.

Perhaps you're feeling overwhelmed - wondering how you will ever feel comfortable eating your meal when you have four forks in front of you and a type of cheese that you've never heard of before! I'm here to assure you that everything is going to be okay. The truth is - etiquette is always evolving, but there is one simple rule that you can always turn to if you need a little bit of guidance.

The Golden Rule is this: Focus on the person, not the plate.

The secret to success at the table is to intentionally connect with the individuals around you! Is it possible that you might use the wrong glass, or lay your napkin down incorrectly at the end of the meal? Of course! But I can guarantee that no one is going to notice a simple mistake like that if they have had a meaningful, uplifting conversation with you during the meal. While learning the rules is important, it's going to take time and practice. Be patient with yourself, and remember to enjoy the company of those around you!
