Meet Janessa Rae

Hey friend, I'm so happy you're here!

At a young age I became fascinated with the rules of etiquette, and I loved exploring the "proper" way to do many things! I learned to set a table with a variety of utensils, I was trained in professional interviewing skills, I practiced communicating with people of all ages, and I thrived in professional settings.

When I turned 19 I had the opportunity to move to Europe for a year and a half, and I was excited to learn that the etiquette differences I had seen in movies were real in Europe! I kept a running list of all of the new etiquette rules I learned while living there, and when I moved home I continued to study etiquette across various countries and cultures!
I am fascinated at the variety of cultural influences that contribute to unique etiquette practices all around the world!

Here are a few fun facts about me:
  1. I am a country girl and love being able to see the stars at night.
  2. My dream is to live in England or Sweden one day.
  3. I love learning languages and understanding cultures from all over the world.
  4. One of my bucket list goals is to ride in a hot air balloon.
