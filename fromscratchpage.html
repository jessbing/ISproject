My goal is to inspire individuals to learn proper etiquette rules across various countries so they can navigate any situation with confidence.
Below is a list of three countries that I have lived in, and a small glimpse into what I learned about etiquette while I was there.

  1. Croatia
    • If you ask for water, you will likely be served sparkling water! Be sure to specify if you don't want that.
    • Meal time should not be rushed, it's a time to talk and enjoy good company.
  2. Serbia
    • A lot of bakeries and restaurants only accept cash.
    • When you are ready to pay for your meal, ask the server for your bill.
  3. America
    • Wait for everyone to be served before you start eating.
    • When you're given a cloth napkin, place it on your lap as you sit down at the table.
